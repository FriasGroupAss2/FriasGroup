Support

Please go through the answered questions below before contacting our Support.

♧ What should I do if the Product reached me in a damaged condition?
If you realize that the packaged product is in a damaged state then please do not accept it and inform us as soon as possible. And if you have already accepted it the please go through our returns section.


♧ Change the delivery address?
You can change your delivery address before your product is shipped by calling our support on +6569999999 and request for an address change.


♧ What happens when I am not available during the delivery hours?
We will try again but it is highly recommended than you plan accordingly to the delivery time.
