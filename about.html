About Frias

FRIAS aims to save the world with its revolutionary eco-friendly clothing which is loved by children. We design and manufacture new trends which shape the world. Our journey started out in 2001 and since then we have been growing rapidly and winning hearts of our dear customers. Our store specializes in eco-friendly clothing with over 200 different designs. All the products are quality controlled and checked before handing out to the customers. We aim to please each and every customer at FRIAS Fashion Outlet.

Kids are the new generation of fashionista and we are dedicated to helping them explore this hobby of their's through our latest trending, variety of exotic products. As our products are eco-friendly this creates a totally new division for them to play around with. This new division not only provides them new trend but it affects the whole world through its eco-friendly matter and it will make a huge difference in the long run.

We are using our reach to be a positive force for our customers and our world. We promote choices and are proud of everything we do. All clothing is made of eco-friendly cotton and clothing brand captures the magical, passionate look and language of children. Frias speaks the language of children. Our core values are creativity, passion and to operate under a social and ecological protocol base on moderation, intelligence and cooperation.

We are committed to delivering your order accurately, in good condition, and always on time.
